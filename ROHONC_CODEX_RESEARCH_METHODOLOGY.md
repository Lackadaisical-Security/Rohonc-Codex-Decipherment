## Overview

The Rohonc Codex is a 448-page illustrated manuscript discovered in Hungary, written in an unknown script with 792 different symbols (10x more than most alphabets). Named after the city of Rohonc (now Rechnitz, Austria), it has defied all decipherment attempts since 1838. The manuscript contains 87 illustrations showing religious, military, and secular scenes.
