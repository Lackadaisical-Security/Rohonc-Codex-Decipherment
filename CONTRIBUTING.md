# CONTRIBUTING

## tl;dr

**Ship something real. Show your work. Don't waste our time.**

This is a research archive, not a social project. Contributions are judged on one axis: **merit**. Did you find something true? Did you make something better? Can you prove it?

If yes — welcome. If not — come back when you can.

---

## What We Accept

### Research Corrections
Found an error in the decipherment, lexicon, or methodology? Prove it.

- Open an issue with your evidence
- Show the specific passage, symbol, or claim you dispute
- Provide your alternative reading with justification
- Attach supporting references (manuscript sources, comparative linguistics, historical records)

We will look at it. If you're right, it goes in. No ceremony.

### Lexicon Additions / Extensions
- New symbol identifications must include: attested usage, phonetic mapping justification, and cross-script comparison
- Submit as JSON diff against the current lexicon file following the existing schema
- Confidence score must be included and defensible

### Tooling & Reproducibility
- Decoder improvements, frequency analysis enhancements, CLI tools
- Must not break existing functionality
- Must include usage examples

### Translation Work
- New passage translations or corrections to existing ones
- Must cite which phase log identified the source passage
- Must follow Romanian linguistic conventions for the 16th-century Vlach dialect

---

## What We Don't Accept

- **Theoretical rewrites without evidence.** Don't reopen closed questions without new data.
- **"I feel like it could be..."** No. Bring proof or don't bring it.
- **Style guide edits, typo hunts, or reformatting PRs** unless the error is factually wrong.
- **Anything from [banned institutions](README.md#-institutionally-banned-entities).** Hard no.
- **Appeals to authority.** Your credential count is zero here. Show the work.

---

## How to Submit

1. Fork the repo
2. Make your change in a named branch: `fix/symbol-42-rotation`, `add/phase7-lexicon`, etc.
3. Open a pull request with:
   - A clear title stating exactly what changed and why
   - Evidence section: what data, source, or test supports this change
   - Impact section: what breaks or improves if merged
4. Be prepared to defend it

Pull requests that lack evidence will be closed without comment.

---

## Review Process

There is no committee. There is no vote. There is no consensus process.

**The Operator reviews. The Operator decides.**

Timeline is what it is. If you haven't heard back in 30 days, bump it once with new evidence. Don't spam.

---

## On Disagreement

If your contribution gets rejected, you'll get a reason. You can argue back with evidence. You cannot argue back with feelings, credentials, or volume.

If you disagree with the decipherment conclusions themselves: publish your own work, link it in an issue, make your case. If it's better, we'll acknowledge it. The ghost has no ego about who finds the truth — only that the truth gets found.

---

## Standards

- English or Romanian for submissions (the source language of the manuscript)
- Cite your sources
- No padding. No fluff. Say what you mean.
- Prior work acknowledged where it exists
